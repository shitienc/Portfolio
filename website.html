Call Me Handy - Startup Mission

About:

In my current study at Zealand, I participated in a project called: Startup Mission, its aim is based on these categories: Greentech, Social Entrepreneurship, LifeTech or Service innovation. Our group developed an idea based on Service innovation, which brought us to Call Me Handy. Call Me Handy is an app for people who need help in regards to various small craft tasks and gardening. They can seek help from experienced people for a smaller price. Since this kind of a product already exists on the market, we chose to optimize our product by including more safety features, which would give us to a stronger position in the market.

My role:

In this project, I was responsable for creating a website for our app and company. It was a demand to create the website from the bottom by using HTML and CSS.

The solution:

My solution for the assignment, was to create an user friendly website, where all the necessary information could be found. I started the process by doing some wireframes and mock ups. This lead to an easy process when I started coding on the website, by using Visual Studio Code.

Den Kinesiske Butik - Business Development

About:

In my previous study AP Degree - Service, Hospitality and Tourism Management at Cphbusiness, I worked on a project called "Business Development". The main focus of this project was to collaborate with an existed company, in which we had to problem solve or optimize an issue with the company. Our group decided to do a collaboration with a retail store called "Den Kinesiske Butik", which sold Chinese and other Asian products. After much internal and external research and analysis of the company, we learned that the company was in need for an optimized and updated website, thus we created a new modern and user-friendly website for Den Kinesiske Butik

My role:

For this project, my main assignment was to design and establish the new website by using the platform WIX. In addition, I was part of the project planning, analysis process and overall problem solution.

The solution:

We created a website which users could easily navigate to find needed information about the store and their products, moreover which products they could buy in the stores webshop. We learned that the target audience were middle age and elderly regular costumers. Thus, the website had to be useful and helpful for this target demographic. The optimized website was also created to be appealing to new younger costumers who desire Asian goods.
