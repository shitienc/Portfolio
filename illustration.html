Traveling Around

About:

I got inspired to create some illustrations that represent me and my interests. In 2019 I backpacked through Asia, and my trip ended up lasting six months. Throughout the trip, I met my boyfriend in Japan, which I chose to illustrate in these photos. In the process of creating these illustrations, I worked with Adobe Illustrator, which gave me a better understanding of how the program works.
