About:

At Zealand, I worked on a project called: SoMe & Branding. Its focus was to get a better understanding of social media, content creation and communication. Our group had to create SoMe content for our previous project: "Call Me Handy" , which lead to my animated GIF's.

My role:

In this project, my role included several different tasks. Although my main task was to create SoMe content and coding a page for the website. Moreover, I participated in the development processes, such as the target group analysis and project writing process.

The solution:

To finish the main task of creating SoMe content, I designed two posts based on the research we had gathered and our target group analysis. After designing the content in Adobe Illustrator, I chose to make the illustration more lively by adding some effects in Adobe After Effects.
